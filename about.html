Our Story

Our first ever family member is an injured community cat named "Sammy". Sammy walked into our classroom in January 2014, abandoned, cold, and hungry. So our class decided to take care of Sammy and Sammy has been our best friend eversince then.

Sammy has been with us in this journey of saving our many friends is now our full time animal therapist. Sammy takes care of other sick animals and gives them emotional support

What is PawVille?

PawVille is made of a group of young animal enthusiasts who are passionate about fighting for the better lives of our fur and feather friends. We believe that every community animals should live a life free from fear and suffering. PawVille employs programs and policies to promote ethical, sustainable and compassionate treatment and the best practice for these animals. We have been helping abandoned animals in Singapore, Batam, and Bintan since June 2015. As of May 2019, we have rescued 1372 friends and found new homes for them.
